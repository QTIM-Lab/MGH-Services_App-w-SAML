Mass General Brigham Community Help

Purpose:

Mass General Brigham Community Help site helps connect colleagues during this extraordinary time. Whether you need assistance or are looking to help a colleague, please read on!

Overview

To get started, simply click the Login button below. You will automatically be logged in via single sign on.

If you need help or would like to volunteer: Please review existing posts. If you find a match for a service you need or one that you would like to offer, please email that person directly.

If you do NOT see a post matching the help you need or are willing to provide, click the green "Post a New Request" for one of the following types of services:

  1. Transportation
  2. Shopping
  3. In-House Help
  4. Pet Sitting/Care

Please note: This site is not the place to request coverage of a hospital shift or to arrange for child care. If you need child care support, please click here.

Please Read

Partners HealthCare is making this site available to employees, but it is not actively monitoring the requests and responses posted. By using this service, you assume all risk and responsibility. Partners is not responsible for the conduct of individuals that choose to participate and receive and/or offer services through Partners HealthCare Community Help. However, if you post inappropriate or illegal requests for services or otherwise communicate in a manner inconsistent with Partners and our organizations standards of professionalism and civility, you may be referred to Human Resources for appropriate action.

It does not request you disclose your house or apartment number for privacy reasons.
