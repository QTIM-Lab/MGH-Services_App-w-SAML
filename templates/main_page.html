MGH Community Help

This site is intended to connect our MGH community and to provide ways for employees who live in the same neighborhood to support each other. Especially with the COVID-19 pendemic some healthcare workers experience greater work-related responsibilities and might require support outside the hospital, such as pet care (e.g. walking the dog), running errands (grocery/pharmacy runs), transportation or help around the house.

If you are interested in requesting/offering support, please fill out the form after you Login. Please fill out the "Post a New Request" form and indicate whether you are Requesting Help or if Can Give Assistance. Please provide your name, address and zip code and the type of help you request or can offer. The initial contact should be through e-mail.
