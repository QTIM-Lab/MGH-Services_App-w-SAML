Tutorial Page

  • Begin by reading the Community Intro and Disclaimer on the home page
  • The actions supported in this app are:
    • Add a Post
    • Update a Post to either "Resolved" or "I dont need help anymore"
    • Click and see your post along with others in the MGH\Brigham Community
    • Sort the results in the table of posts
    • Search posts for key words or phrases to help you find help or fins someone to help
  • Remember to hit refresh to see your pin show up on the map
  • You will not be able to retrieve a post if you mark it as "Resolved" or "I dont need help anymore"
  • There are 4 types of help available:
    • Pet Care
    • In House Care
    • Transportation Help
    • Shopping Help
  • If you choose to "help" vs receive help then your icon shows up as a red heart
  • Lastly if posts and or icons aren't showing up, try hitting 'ctrl+shift+r' or doing a hard refresh.
